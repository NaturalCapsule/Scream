SCREAM (1996)

Scream (1996) is a slasher horror film about a masked killer, dubbed Ghostface, who terrorizes high school students in the town of Woodsboro, California, by using horror movie rules in a deadly game. The story follows teenager Sidney Prescott as she and her friends are targeted, forcing them to use their knowledge of horror films to uncover the killer's identity before they become the next victims

Director: Wes Craven

Genres:

    • Horror
    • Slasher
    • Action
    • Mystery
    • Thriller

Main Plot

After a series of murders in the town, Sidney and her friends realize the killer is using horror movie tropes to play a deadly game with them.
Sidney is particularly targeted because-
the murders seem connected to the anniversary of her mother's death a year prior

Sidney Prescott (Main Character)

Sidney Prescott is a 17-year-old high school student from Woodsboro, California. She’s smart, brave, and just trying to live a normal life, even while still dealing with the trauma of losing her mother. Everything changes when the Ghostface killer starts targeting her, forcing her to fight for her life and protect the people she cares about.

Billy Loomis

Billy Loomis is a quiet and mysterious high school student from Woodsboro who’s closely connected to Sidney Prescott. He carries a calm, collected attitude with an edge of unpredictability, making him stand out among their group of friends.

Stu Macher

Stu Macher is an energetic and outgoing high school student from Woodsboro. He’s the loud, joking member of the friend group, always trying to keep things fun, even when situations get tense. His playful personality and wild sense of humor make him memorable, but also a bit unpredictable at times.
